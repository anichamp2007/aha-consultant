Visa Services


Things to consider

How to apply:

An applicant can apply ONLINE through the respective countries immigration website or OFFLINE through designated consulate/ High Commission or VFS office.


Types of Visa

1. Tourist Visa
2. Business Visa
3. Medical Visa
4. Student Visa
5. Work Visa
6. Immigrant Visa
7. Transit Visa
8. VoA (Pre-Arrival Registration)

where each and every types is having its own specific document requirements in order to be eligible for Visa Application


Visa Requirements

There are various types of visas, and one of the biggest requirements of holding any of these visas is that they particularly meet the eligibility criteria,

1. Passport-must be at least 6 months of validity a time of application
2. Health & Character Requirements as per the Regulation
3. Must hold strong financial status with appropriate bank balance
4. Other supportive requirements are as per the visiting coutry guidline.


Visa Process

An applicant will have to fill the application form through a visa service provider whith supporting documents or through an online consulate website where individual and all accompaning has to fill the forems and upload the necessary supporting document and pay visa fees in order for the consulate to process the application.


Visa Fees

Fees vary with respect to country, number of entries (single-double-multiple), duration of stay you are applying. Please check with your visa service provider for details


Processing Time

Some consulate/ Embassay process the application same day which can vary to 30-45 days depends on the partcular visa type you are applying for.


Visa Validity

Upon granting the visa application, the applicant will receive the sticker/ print visa which mentions the start date, end date, number of entries you can avail. Some country allows you for 15-30 days in case of VoA, 1-6 months or more than 12 months depends on type of visa you applied.


Visa Condition

Subject to type of visa you apply
